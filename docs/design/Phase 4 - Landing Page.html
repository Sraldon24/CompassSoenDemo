Features

Everything the registrar's site doesn't tell you.

Six tools, one workspace. Compass replaces a tab full of PDFs, a spreadsheet of guesses, and three texts to your advisor.

Term Planner

Drag courses across Fall, Winter, and Summer. Prereqs validated in real time. Drop is rejected if the move breaks your degree.

Prereq Map

The whole SOEN program as an interactive graph. Click a course to see what it unlocks and what it requires — three clicks deep.

Workload Predictor

Each term is scored Light → Burnout based on credits, assignment load, and historical course difficulty. Plan a term you can survive.

Ask Compass

Chat with an AI advisor trained on the Concordia calendar, your transcript, and your plan. Every answer cites its sources.

Requirements Tracker

120 credits, five categories, one progress bar. See what's missing at a glance — and what one extra elective could unlock.

Public Profile

Share your plan at compass.dev/u/your-name. Recruiter-friendly snapshot of where you are in the program.
