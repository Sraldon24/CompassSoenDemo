Can I take SOEN 341 in Fall 2026 if I haven't done ENGR 201 yet? My friend said it's not a hard prereq.

C Compass 14:32 · 1.8s

Your friend is half-right. Technically ENGR 201 is a hard prereq for SOEN 341 — it's listed under §71.20.10 of the SE program calendar1, and the registrar will block enrollment without it.

But here's the practical part: the department has been known to grant a permission override if you take ENGR 201 concurrently and have a B+ in COMP 248/2492. You qualify on both counts (you got an A−).

Realistically you have two paths:

  • Take ENGR 201 in Summer 2026 (currently empty in your plan), then SOEN 341 in Fall as planned. Workload stays at Heavy.
  • Swap SOEN 341 for COMP 348 in Fall — both fit the 4th-semester slot and don't depend on ENGR 201. Your Fall workload drops to Medium.
