Can I take SOEN 341 in Fall 2026 if I haven't done ENGR 201 yet?

C Compass

Technically no — ENGR 201 is a hard prereq for SOEN 341. The Concordia calendar lists it under "Section 71.20.10" of the SE program.

Two realistic options: (1) Take ENGR 201 in Summer 2026, then SOEN 341 in Fall as planned. (2) Swap SOEN 341 for COMP 348 in Fall — both fit the 4th-semester slot and don't depend on ENGR 201.

If you're set on Fall, option 2 keeps your workload lighter (you'd be at Medium instead of Heavy).
